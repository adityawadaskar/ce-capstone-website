Execution Instructions:
1. Insert high definition logos of sponsors into the "sponsors_original" directory.
2. Execute the "format_images.sh" script by typing ./format_images.sh in the command line.
3. Add the new sponsor images into the DB.

What format_images.sh does:
1. Converts all image formats to .png and removes non .png files.
2. Creates small and large image sizes that are consistent in size.
