1. Insert high definition logos of current sponsors into the "current_sponsors" directory.
2. Execute the "format_images.sh" script by typing ./format_images.sh in the command line.

The format_images.sh script does the following things:
1. Converts all image formats to .png and removes non .png files.
2. Creates icon sized images for the sponsor carousel on the home screen.
3. Appends the contents of current_sponsors to all_sponsors and current_sponsors_resized to all_sponsors_resized.
